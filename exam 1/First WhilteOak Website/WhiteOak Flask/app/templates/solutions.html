Multimedia, Scientific

Chapel Hill Pediatric Speech Pathology helps children develop the skills necessary for effective communication using qualitative and quantitative assessment methods to analyze and diagnose the nature of the communication impairment. Their quantitative methods often employ audio analysis and transcription. Our client was spending hours hitting rewind & play on a tape recorder.

We proposed upgrading to digital recording and editing tools. After a review the available audio recording and transcription software, we developed a simple solution using Audacity (link) for audio recording and audio/video playback. We researched microphones and webcams to find the most suitable and cost effective. As necessary, we provided oversight of software/hardware installation, and instruction in new editing techniques.

White Oak IT provides complete end-to-end solutions:

Hardware -> Software -> Solution Development -> Installation -> Training
